Classic

Complete your duties before the duck(s) sabotage or kill enough geese to end the mission. Figure out the identities of the ducks and vote to remove them.

Draft

Draft mode is a variation of Classic where you are assigned a hidden player number. On your turn, choose between one of three roles at the start of the game, or select a random role. This is recommended for advanced players who are familiar with classic.

Goosehunt

One killer duck is on the loose trying to kill everyone else before time runs out. Geese can complete tasks to reduce the timer or choose to hide. Completing a task reduces the timer by 4 seconds while alive and 1 second while dead. In the last 60 seconds of the game, the duck gets a boost in speed.

Dine and Dash

The red team of morphing ducks are trying to kill the blue team before the timer runs out. The blue team is a vulture that reduces the timer by eating corpse and is partnered with a falcon who kills faster than the ducks in games with more than 6 players. Recommended for private games with 10 players or more and proximity chat.

Trick or Treat

Villagers complete tasks to call meetings. Vote out the monster to win. The monster wins when everyone's been converted to a thrall. Thralls are powered up by the monster's abilities and win with the monster.

Hanging out

Make friends, chat and play mini-games. Mini-games will be released over time so check back often to see what else we've got cooking up!
