Goose, goose, DUCK? Goose, goose, DUCK? A game of social deduction where you and your fellow geese must work together to complete your mission. Keep an eye out for those malicious Mallards and other birds, who have infiltrated your team and will do anything to stop you.

This Game Includes:

Private and Public Game Lobbies.

Built-in proximity chat

Loads of Cosmetics to customize your goose! Hats, Shirts, Farts, Pets!

7+ maps including S.S. Mothergoose, Mallard Manor, Jungle Temple, The Basement, and Ancient Sands!

5+ built-in game modes including Classic, Draft, Trick or Treat, Dine & Dash, and Goose Hunt!

45+ optional sub-roles including the Dodo Bird, the Pelican, and the Morphling!
