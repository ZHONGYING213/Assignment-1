Special Roles

Duck

As a Duck, your objective is the opposite: your job is to sabotage the ship, kill the other Goose players stealthily, and convince other players to vote off anyone but you (and the other Ducks if you’re working together). Ducks can also use the ventilation shafts around the airship.

But using these vents and being spotted murdering a Goose or destroying life support right in the open are dead giveaways. So you’ll need to pick your spots carefully and play along with the other Geese until the time is right for some betrayal.

Special Roles

Cannibal

You can eat a corpse once per game.

Morphling

You can shapeshift into other players.

Silencer

You can mute other players during meetings but you can't vent.

Professional

Geese can not see your victims and will auto-report them when they get near.

Spy

Being the only person to vote for someone reveals their role.

Assassin

Twice per game, kill during meetings by correctly guessing a role or die trying.

Snitch

Being the only person to vote for someone puts them in jail.

Demolitionist

You plant bombs on other players.

Party

You give other players a high pitched voice during meetings.

Identity Thief

You shapeshift into your most recent victim until a meeting is called.

Ninja

You can kill two people in range simultaneously, but your kill cooldown is triple.

Undertaker

You can drag bodies at a reduced speed.

Invisibility

You can turn invisible.

Serial Killer

Killing your target reduces your cooldown. Killing others increases it.

Warlock

Summons a deadly swarm of locusts as its sabotage.

Esper

You can talk to and kill players remotely.

Hitman

Kill to get a bonus
